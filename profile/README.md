<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Plot</h2>
<p>The story follows a mysterious &ldquo;traveller&rdquo; who infiltrates the underworld of Karachi. He rises through its ranks with precision and cunning, only to dismantle the dangerous nexus between the ISI and organized crime. The narrative is filled with espionage, betrayal, and high-octane action sequences.</p>
<p>Ranveer Singh&rsquo;s character is central to the mission, balancing intelligence and brute force. The film explores themes of patriotism, sacrifice, and the hidden battles fought by agents in the shadows. With daring missions across borders, the plot builds towards a tense climax where the protagonist must risk everything to protect national security.</p>
